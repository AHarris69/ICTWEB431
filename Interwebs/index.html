About Us

Massive Games is a creative community of artists and game makers located in Victoria, Australia. Massive Games started as a group of independent and freelance game developers looking for a shared place. In early 2013, our not - for - profit game studio was founded.

We're multi-disciplined with skills in multiple fields. Our projects are run independently, but may involve multiple members working together. Over time, we've grown into an event and community hub for the indie games community for regional Victoria.
